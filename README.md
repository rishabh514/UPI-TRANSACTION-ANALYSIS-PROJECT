# UPI Ecosystem Intelligence Dashboard (2016–2025)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the evolution of India's Unified Payments Interface (UPI) ecosystem between 2016 and 2025 using SQL and Power BI.

Rather than focusing only on transaction growth, the project evaluates the ecosystem from multiple business perspectives including market concentration, competitive dynamics, geographic adoption, operational stability, and long-term growth resilience.

The dashboard was built by transforming raw datasets using SQL and developing a six-page analytical Power BI dashboard that answers key business and strategic questions relevant to digital payments, fintech, and platform ecosystems.

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview

**File:** `dashboard-screenshots/01_executive_overview.png`

Provides a high-level summary of the entire UPI ecosystem by combining transaction growth, market concentration, and operational stability into a single executive dashboard.

This page is intended for decision-makers who need a quick assessment of ecosystem health without reviewing detailed reports.

#### Key Metrics

- Latest Monthly Volume: **15,650 Mn**
- Market Concentration (HHI): **0.36**
- Latest YoY Growth: **53.03%**
- Negative Growth Events
- Long-Term Transaction Trend

#### Business Relevance

This view helps stakeholders understand whether ecosystem growth is being driven by healthy adoption, whether competition remains balanced, and whether operational stability is improving over time.

#### Key Findings

- UPI transactions increased from **0.13 million monthly transactions in August 2016** to over **15.6 billion monthly transactions by February 2025**.
- Latest YoY growth reached approximately **53%**.
- Despite strong long-term expansion, the ecosystem experienced **21 negative-growth months**.
- Market concentration remains moderate with an HHI of **0.36**, indicating the continued importance of competitive diversification as transaction volumes scale.

### 2. App Market Share Analysis

**File:** `dashboard-screenshots/02_app_market_share_analysis.png`

Analyzes market share evolution and competitive positioning across major UPI applications including PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, CRED, BHIM, Amazon Pay, and WhatsApp Pay.

#### Key Metrics

- Largest App Market Share
- Top 2 Combined Market Share
- App Rankings
- Monthly Share Changes
- Market Share Evolution

#### Business Relevance

Understanding market concentration is critical for fintech companies, investors, regulators, and payment platforms. High concentration often indicates strong network effects and increased barriers for new entrants.

#### Key Findings

- PhonePe holds approximately **48.5%** market share.
- Google Pay holds approximately **34.9%** market share.
- The top two players collectively control approximately **83.4%** of transaction volume.
- While the number of major apps increased from four to seven between 2016 and 2025, leadership positions remained largely unchanged.
- Smaller players continue to struggle to achieve meaningful scale despite ecosystem growth.

### 3. State Adoption Analysis

**File:** `dashboard-screenshots/03_state_adoption_analysis.png`

Examines geographic adoption patterns using state-level transaction volume and per-capita usage metrics.

The page combines ranking analysis, regional comparisons, and map visualizations to identify adoption disparities across India.

#### Key Metrics

- State Transaction Volume
- Per-Capita UPI Usage
- Highest State Volume
- Highest Per-Capita Usage
- Geographic Distribution

#### Business Relevance

For fintech companies and payment providers, understanding geographic concentration helps prioritize expansion, marketing investments, merchant acquisition efforts, and infrastructure deployment.

#### Key Findings

- Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Delhi collectively contribute approximately **37%** of total transaction volume.
- The top five states account for approximately **51.4%** of all transactions.
- The top ten states contribute approximately **76.2%** of ecosystem volume.
- Adoption remains heavily concentrated in a limited number of regions despite nationwide availability.
- Significant growth opportunities remain in lower-adoption states.

### 5. Growth Resilience Analysis

**File:** `dashboard-screenshots/05_growth_resilience_analysis.png`

Measures how resilient the UPI ecosystem remained during periods of contraction, volatility, and changing growth conditions.

The page focuses on negative-growth events, volatility patterns, and long-term stability indicators.

#### Key Metrics

- Negative Growth Months
- Average Monthly Volatility
- Largest Monthly Decline

#### Business Relevance

Rapid growth alone does not indicate ecosystem strength. Sustainable ecosystems must demonstrate resilience during shocks, contractions, and changing market conditions.

#### Key Findings

- The ecosystem experienced **21 negative-growth months** between 2016 and 2025.
- The largest monthly decline reached approximately **19.8%**.
- Average monthly volatility measured approximately **15.74%**.
- Growth volatility declined significantly over time.
- Despite occasional contractions, transaction volumes continued expanding over the long term, indicating increasing maturity and resilience.

## Key Insights

### Rapid Ecosystem Expansion

UPI transaction volume increased from approximately **0.13 million monthly transactions in 2016** to over **15.6 billion monthly transactions by 2025**, representing one of the fastest digital payment adoption curves globally.

### Strong Market Concentration

Despite increasing ecosystem participation, market leadership remains highly concentrated.

- PhonePe controls approximately **48.5%** market share.
- The top two players control approximately **83.4%** of transaction volume.
- Market concentration remains elevated with an HHI of **0.36**.

This suggests strong network effects and meaningful barriers to scale for smaller competitors.

### Geographic Adoption Gap

UPI adoption remains uneven across India.

- Top five states contribute over half of all transaction activity.
- Top ten states account for more than three-quarters of total volume.
- Large adoption gaps continue to exist between leading and lower-usage regions.

### Increasing Operational Stability

Although the ecosystem experienced **21 negative-growth months**, long-term transaction growth remained uninterrupted.

Volatility has declined substantially since the early years of UPI adoption, indicating increasing maturity and operational resilience.

### Stable Competitive Leadership

While new applications entered the ecosystem over time, competitive leadership remained largely stable. Scale advantages and network effects continue to play a major role in shaping market outcomes.

## SQL Transformations

The dashboard was built using SQL-generated analytical datasets rather than directly visualizing raw files.

Implemented SQL techniques include:

- Window Functions (`LAG`)
- Ranking Functions (`RANK`)
- Rolling Averages
- Year-over-Year Growth Calculations
- Quarterly Aggregations
- Market Share Analysis
- Anomaly Detection Logic
- State-Level Aggregations

The SQL scripts used to generate analytical datasets are available in the `sql-queries` folder.
